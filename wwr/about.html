Fischetti Rafting Co.

At Fischetti Rafting Co., we believe the river is more than water—it’s a teacher, healer, and endless adventure. Our mission is to create unforgettable experiences that inspire a deeper connection to nature and ignite a spirit of exploration in everyone who joins us on the water. We’re passionate about sharing the transformative power of rivers, fostering teamwork, and creating memories that last a lifetime. Join us to discover the thrill of the rapids and the serenity of the wild.

History

In the heart of the rugged wilderness, where roaring rivers carve through ancient forests and misty mountains, the story of Fischetti Rafting Co. began. Founded in 1905 by Rosalia Fischetti, a passionate adventurer with a love for the untamed waters, our company was born out of a simple dream: to share the thrill of the rapids with the world. Nazzareno, a third-generation Italian immigrant, grew up listening to his grandmother's tales of sailing across the Mediterranean.

With nothing but a single raft, a handful of paddles, and an unshakable determination, Nazzareno started Fischetti Rafting Co. in a small riverside cabin. Word of his expertly guided trips and infectious enthusiasm spread quickly, and soon, adventurers from all over flocked to experience the thrill of the rapids with Nazzareno and his team. Over the decades, Fischetti Rafting Co. has grown into a family legacy.
